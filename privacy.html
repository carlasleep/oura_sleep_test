Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how oura_sleep_test collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

This application collects the following information from the Oura Cloud API. All information is collected with your explicit consent.

  • **Oura Ring Data**: Sleep stages (deep sleep, REM, light sleep, wake time), heart rate, activity, sleep scores, etc.

2. Purpose of Use

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To analyze your sleep patterns and provide visualized reports.
  • To offer statistical insights into your health status.

3. Data Storage and Security

This application **stores data only on your local device**. It does not transmit or store any data on external servers or cloud services. The data is stored unencrypted on your device. By storing data locally and avoiding external servers, we aim to protect your data from unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing

This application **does not share or sell** any of your collected data with any third parties without your explicit consent.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • **Access and Deletion**: You can delete all locally stored data by simply deleting the application from your device.
